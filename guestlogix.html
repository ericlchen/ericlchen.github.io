Guestlogix

In-Flight Merchandise Ordering App

Context

Guestlogix is a mobile platform that allows passengers to order merchandise before or during their flight. During my internship at TribalScale, I had the opportunity to work on the redesign of the iOS app for passengers. During the design phase, I was responsible for creating majority of the user flows, screens, and assets, as well as compiling an InVision prototype. During the development phase, I assisted the developers by making assets and managing the Zeplin library. The team consisted of a product manager, two developers, and two designers.

Process

We were provided with user research done prior to the inception of the project, which we used to guide our designs. By utilizing a familiar e-commerce framework, we were able to focus on improving key experience points: priority boarding passes and the customer checkout flow. The priority boarding pass was reimagined as an Apple Wallet ticket and the checkout flow was designed to guide users through a stepped process. We held weekly in-person design reviews with the Guestlogix team where I was responsible for walking the client through our design decisions and prototype.
