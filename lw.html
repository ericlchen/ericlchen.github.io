Losers Weepers Vintage

Identity and Merchandise Design

Losers Weepers is an online vintage clothing store based in Kingston, Ontario. I was hired as an in-house graphic designer to create a new identity and merchandise for the brand. Inspiration and references are drawn from the 1960s psychedelic art movement.
