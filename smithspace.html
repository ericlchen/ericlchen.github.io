SmithSPACE

Conceptual iOS Room Booking App

Context

SmithSPACE is the room booking service for students and staff at the Smith School of Business. I was commissioned to lead a pro-bono design project in 2018 for the Commerce Society Agency to design a concept app to replace the current web experience. I worked with two junior designers to conduct research and design a prototype to reimagine the booking process for students.

Process

We conducted interviews with multiple student groups in the school and synthesized our research into a journey map which we used to guide our design. We focused on improving the experience of choosing dates by replacing the current dropdown menu with a calendar modal. We also reimagined the one-page web experience by breaking the overall process into many steps which helped reduce the cognitive load on the user.
