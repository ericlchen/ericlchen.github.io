Work

Impact Tomorrow

Founder, Executive Director, Board Member • September 2013 - Present

I co-founded Impact Tomorrow to promote entrepreneurship to the youth of my community. I took responsibility of getting monetary sponsorship, reaching out to speakers, and marketing our events on social media.
I led the grassroots organization to recruit additional executive members, including a project manager and a graphic designer.
In March 2015, I planned and hosted the 2nd annual Impact Tomorrow Expo, and it featured a set of startup-driven workshops. It also featured the LAUNCH Pitch Competition which provided funding to top youth-led startups.
Shortly after the conference, I registered Impact Tomorrow as a non-profit organization with the CRA. I now sit on the Board of Directors for Impact Tomorrow. This year, we are planning to branch out to host 3 mini-events in the Peel Region, Guelph, and Toronto.

Impact Tomorrow Expo 2015

I planned a full-day conference, helping high school students discover more about startups and entrepreneurship. The conference featured a set of workshops based on a toolkit I conceptualized called the Entrepreneurial Method. A total of 150 youth were led through the workshops where they created an idea for a "green-energy" themed product, developed the business model and pitched it to industry leaders. The conference featured a larger pitch competition at the end where three groups of youth won prize money to get their startup ideas funded.
