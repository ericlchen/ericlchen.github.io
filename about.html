about me

I’m a second year student studying commerce at Queen’s University. I aspire to be a great designer and product manager that can solve problems by crafting delightful digital experiences. I chose to pursue a career in design rather than a typical business pathway because I like building products that people love using!

In my free time, I love finding new ways to express my creativity. In high school, I used to be part of a breakdance crew. I now enjoy listening to R&B, hip-hop, and indie music as I decrypt lyrics on Genius — you’ll catch me listening to Frank Ocean on a daily. I also enjoy keeping up with tech news and design trends on Medium, reading design books, and analyzing apps that I use.
