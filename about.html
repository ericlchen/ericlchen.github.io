Resume

Hey, I'm Eric.

There is nothing I enjoy more in life than creating things.

In high school, I ran a youth entrepreneurship conference, which sparked my interest in the tech and startup space. I had dreams of working for and eventually build my own startup. But after I started business school, I quickly realized none of the traditional career paths fit me. I knew I had an innate affinity for design, so I taught myself the basics of UI + UX and started seeking out work.

After three years, I have accumulated experience designing for startups and other larger clients such as CIBC, Radio.com, Guestlogix, and Guardian Analytics.

In my spare time I always try to keep myself creatively stimulated. I practice film photography and use my photos to design clothes, magazines, album cover art, and posters.

I’m currently looking for full-time work once I graduate in April 2019.
