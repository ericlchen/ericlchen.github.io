Work About Resume

There is nothing I enjoy more than creating things.

I am a self-taught designer and student from Smith School of Business at Queen’s University.

I once had dreams of working for and eventually building my own startup. But after I started business school, I quickly realized none of the traditional career paths fit me. I knew I had an innate affinity for design, so I taught myself the basics of UI/UX and started seeking out work.

After three years, I have accumulated experience designing for startups and other larger clients such as CIBC, Radio.com, Guestlogix, and Guardian Analytics.

In my spare time I always try to keep myself creatively stimulated. I practice film photography and use my photos to design clothes, magazines, album cover art, and posters.

I am currently looking for full-time work once I graduate in April 2019.
