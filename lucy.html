LUCY

Conceptual Android AR Interior Design App

Context

LUCY is a mobile app conceptualized by the Queen’s Technology and Media Association made to help people virtually decorate their rooms using augmented reality technology. I was hired by the LUCY team as a design consultant to build an Android app prototype that simulates the experience of a user uploading a photo, placing a piece of furniture, capturing, and sharing the image.

Process

The LUCY team provided me some direction—they wanted an experience that mimicked Snapchat but still followed Google’s Material Design guidelines. I chose to use a similar image capture button as Snapchat and conventional Material Design elements such as the search bar to make the experience feel familiar for users. I also added a small brand mark in the corner of captured images as a way for LUCY to gain awareness.
